# Privacy Policy for Your Browsing Analytics

**Last updated:** January 11, 2026

## Overview

"Your Browsing Analytics" is a Chrome browser extension that provides visualizations and statistics about your browsing history. This privacy policy explains how the extension handles your data.

## Data Collection

### What data we access

The extension accesses the following data:

- **Browsing history**: URLs, page titles, and visit timestamps from your browser's history

### What data we store

- **Cached analytics**: Processed statistics are cached locally using Chrome's storage API to improve performance
- **User preferences**: Your settings and preferences are stored locally

## Data Usage

Your browsing history data is used **exclusively** to:

- Generate statistics about your most visited websites
- Create visualizations of your browsing activity over time
- Display activity patterns by time of day
- Show domain distribution charts

## Data Storage and Security

- **All data stays on your device**: The extension processes your browsing history entirely locally on your computer
- **No external servers**: No data is ever transmitted to external servers or third parties
- **No cloud storage**: Your data is never uploaded to the cloud
- **No analytics or tracking**: The extension does not include any analytics, telemetry, or tracking code

## Data Sharing

**We do not share your data with anyone.**

- We do not sell your data
- We do not transfer your data to third parties
- We do not use your data for advertising
- We do not use your data for creditworthiness or lending purposes

## Permissions Explained

| Permission | Why it's needed |
|------------|-----------------|
| `history` | To read your browsing history and generate analytics |
| `storage` | To save your preferences and cache processed data locally |
| `tabs` | To open the analytics dashboard in a new tab |

## Your Control

- You can uninstall the extension at any time, which will remove all stored preferences and cached data
- You can clear your browser history at any time through Chrome's settings
- The extension only reads history data; it never modifies or deletes your browsing history

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this document.
